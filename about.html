Who We Are

Founded in 2008, Helping Hands has grown into a trusted community organisation offering temporary shelter, meals, and educational support to hundreds of children each year.

Vision: A world where every child has a safe home, nutritious meals, and opportunities to achieve their dreams.

Mission: To provide food, shelter, and hope for underprivileged children by meeting basic needs and empowering them through learning.
